About Pawtel

A pet-friendly web developed using dom parser for web scraping technique. It is a platform for travelers with pets to find pet-friendly hotels in Malaysia.

Problem Statement

Pet-related products and services are still in the early stage of development.
The major reason for people to travel with pet is solely because of separation anxiety since 70% of pet owners would bring souvenirs for their pets,
while 40% of them said that it is difficult to leave pets for a week than their significant other.

Behind Pawtel

The word "Paw" refers to the foot of an animal while the word "Tel" derive from the word "Hotel" as in accommodation.
